Crowd-Funding With Bitcoin (LinkedIn Post)

No crowd-funding effort is to small for Crowd-Funding with Bitcoin. It does not matter if you collect funds for your next multi-billion business idea or the neighbourhood grill party.

Currently crowd-funding gets a lot of traction and that not only in the entrepreneurial space. No wonder, it allows to get money directly from the community interested in the project or business idea that is crowd-funded. Additional to the funds, the target market analysis is for free and a community can develop around the project or company.

One of the downsides of current crowd-funding services are the high amount of fees and that they are not tailored to smaller funding campaigns. Collecting money from your fellow peers for the next grill party or for a birthday gift is not really feasible, nor recommended with classical crowd-funding services.

Don’t be sad, Crowd-Funding with Bitcoin can be exactly what you have looked for. Bitcoin is a digital currency that has gained momentum in the last few months. For all of you that have missed the Bitcoin hype, checkout the video under bitcoin.org

Why Crowd-Funding with Bitcoin is perfect for your next group activity?

It is without doubt the easiest and fastest way to fund group activities like trips or events where everybody finances a part.

  • It is not getting much easier. Paying is as easy as making a photo.
  • Traceable without privacy concerns. Has everybody paid the promised amount? Get notified or check if you have reached your goal. Not only for the whole campaign, but on a personal level (only by the crowd-funding campaign initiator).
  • Cheaper as nearly all current solutions. Through the nature of Bitcoin transactions are cheap and can be used for micro payments too. No high and artificial service fees apply.
  • Faster as nearly all current solutions. Somebody has forgotten to pay a few minutes before the event starts? No problem. One transaction takes only a few minutes.
  • It is your money from the start. Cryptography makes it possible that the received funds are only accessible by you. Not even the crowd-funding service can access them.

Bitcoin as new technology and financial means will bring crowd-funding to everybody and simplify the process.
