A Day in My Life as an Entrepreneur:
It Is Getting Serious...

A lot has happened since the year 2013 has gone to an end. Sigimera is now more active and more publicly present. I have reached out to different parties and started hosting different events. We have started our pilot phase with our new service Sigimera Fundraising. Also if you are not interested in fundraising you can use it to get familiar with Bitcoin.

During this pilot phase and the related events we got useful feedback that we incorporate stepwise in the current pilot. And if that is not enough we have started to work seriously on a backend model that spreads across the fields of finance, social studies, economy, computer science and even neural science. It is hard to say in this early stage, but as simple as it will be for our users as sophisticated it will be in background. We believe that fundraising as it is now is not good enough and could be improved in an essential way. That will be my mission for at least the next few months, but more likely the next few years.

Sigimera is ready to get serious and provide you with a service that will change your perspective how you view fundraising and campaigns. Stay tuned for more information or even better contact me directly. Looking forward to it!
