A Day in My Life as an Entrepreneur:
The Beginning

This is the first post in the series A day in my life as an entrepreneur. In this series I will share my experience how it feels to be an entrepreneur and what challenges we face in our day-to-day work. This is basically the story of my company Sigimera Ltd. and how we survive outside the big startup hubs. Although we profit from the current startup hype around the world we are currently not directly part of it. But lets start from the beginning a few years ago…

All started 5 years ago during a dark and stormy winter night in an even darker cellar… No, just kidding! The time span ago is approximately true, although it is always hard to say, because you don’t wake up and say: “Today I start my new life as entrepreneur!”. The rest is made up. Not all stereotypes are true. Nevertheless… Around this time I have started my first smaller projects and a short time period after, it was clear that I want to start my own company, in order to professionally support this projects. I should mention that I have a background in business and studied at that time computer science at the University of Innsbruck. This is a powerful combination that helps, but is not needed, so don’t be afraid. During this time I have exploited different technologies and switched pretty often between projects. The time was not right for the one big project to rule them all. This was the first phase were I have learned my craft and invested a lot of time to get the basic right and this approach has never changed. Never stop learning!!! Additional it was useful that I owned the domain networld.to and one server. With this basic infrastructure I was able to setup a first working environment used for the software development and get a feeling about workflows and what works and what doesn’t for our team.

Now that I have bored you enough let me know in the comments what you expect from such a series of blog post. Should I continue in chronologically order? Or should I move to the present and write from time to time flashbacks? Do you want know something special? Every feedback is really appreciated.
