A Day in My Life as an Entrepreneur:
The Year 2013...

The year is nearly finished that means it is a good time to look back and evaluate and asses the achievements. 2013 was an amazing year with a lot of changes and visible results, but also with a lot of challenges and hard work. The following list outlines the most important and biggest milestones this year.

1. Milestone: New Product ‘Campaign Management with Bitcoins’

With the beginning of this year we have started with a new idea to improve donations with the help of bitcoins. In the meantime we have left the prototype phase and working now on a first minimum viable product aka. MVP. This MVP will be used for the pilot phase, with selected users, starting in the first quarter of 2014. The new product is not only usable for donations, but also for all other money acquisition or transaction handling tasks. Stay tuned for this service. It will change the way you think about donations, crowd-funding and related campaign management.

2. Milestone: Sigimera Ltd. was founded

The new product from the first milestone (see above) has resulted in the company formation of Sigimera Ltd. This step should improve trust in our products, but also allows us to provide sophisticated support and services to business and private customers. Sigimera Ltd. will also develop the Crises Information Platform further and combine it with our other services.

3. Milestone: Sigimera expands to Amsterdam

My personal move to Amsterdam, Netherlands had the purpose to push the company, but also to continue my studies at the VU Amsterdam. The main motivation behind the decision was not only to push the company, but also to promote our products and services. For me personally that means that I search now actively for cooperation’s and that I improve my academic, but also my soft-skills.

Do you want to reach Sigimera in Amsterdam? Please feel free to contact me personally.

4. Milestones: Sigimera Ltd. Cooperation’s

Especially to mention this year is that we have cooperated with different external parties and will continue this next year. For example the Windows Phone App for our Crises Information Platform was developed by a Software Engineer from the Microsoft Student Partner program (thank you Asma Bhatti). Additional a student team at the VU Amsterdam works currently together with us on a channel recommendation algorithm for our Campaign Management with Bitcoins product. Also not to forget are the different startups and private persons that participate in our upcoming pilot phase. Thank you guys!

I wish you all nice holidays and a good new year.
